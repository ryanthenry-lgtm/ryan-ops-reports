1. Introduction

Sophie ("we," "us," or "our") operates as a bar and restaurant in Houston, Texas. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with us, including through our SMS notification service.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Phone number — to send you SMS confirmations, reminders, and updates.
  • Name — to personalize your experience and manage reservations.
  • Reservation information — including date, time, party size, and any special requests.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Sending SMS booking confirmations and reminders for your reservations.
  • Providing operational updates (e.g., hours changes, reservation modifications).
  • Responding to your inquiries or support requests.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your personal information to any third parties. Your data is never used for marketing purposes beyond communications you have explicitly opted into.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
